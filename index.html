Marvel Meets A24

Where Intimacy Meets Infinity

A design experiment exploring what would happen if Marvel was owned by A24 - transforming superhero narratives through an indie lens.

The Concept

What if the Marvel Cinematic Universe was reimagined through A24's distinctive lens? This project explores the intersection of blockbuster superhero storytelling and intimate, character-driven narratives that A24 is known for.

Through careful study of A24's visual language - their use of atmospheric lighting, minimalist typography, and emotional depth - I've created a series of movie posters that transform familiar Marvel characters into something entirely new.

Design Process

01

Research & Analysis

I began by studying A24's visual identity - their use of atmospheric photography, minimalist typography, and focus on human emotion over spectacle. I analyzed how they transform genre films into intimate character studies.

02

Concept Development

The key insight was shifting focus from action to emotion. Instead of "Avengers: Endgame," we get "Odinson" - a meditation on loss and identity. Each poster strips away the bombast to reveal the human core.

03

Visual Execution

Using A24's signature aesthetic - muted color palettes, atmospheric lighting, and clean typography - I created posters that feel both familiar and completely transformed. The Marvel logo itself becomes more intimate and approachable.

Mood Board & References

The Netflix mockup shows how the Odinson concept would appear on the streaming platform, demonstrating the versatility of the A24 aesthetic across different media formats.
